About the School

Borough of Manhattan Community College or BMCC is a 2 year University that is part of the CUNY (City University of New York) system.
BMCC aims to provide accessible and high-quality education for everyone.
BMCC emphasizes on inclusivity and empowering indivials to reach their full potential.
BMCC has 3 buildings, the main campus, the murray building and the fiterman hall. They are located at 199 Chamber Street (Main Campus), 70 murray street (Murray Building) and 245 Greenwich street (Fiterman Hall).
